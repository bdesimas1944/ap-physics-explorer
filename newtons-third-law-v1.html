Newton's Third Law: the force pair only exists during the collision, this is a very short time span. Before and after the collision, only Newton's First Law is present — objects in motion stay in motion, objects at rest stay at rest, unless a force acts on them. NOTE: For this reason, a book on a table is not an example of Newton's Third Law.
